CRM Module Development Scope

The CRM module should manage the full sales pipeline and customer relationship process from lead creation until conversion, follow-up, handover, and retention. It should also support communication channels like email and WhatsApp, internal notifications, automatic reminders, and lead or task transfer between teams.

Lead Flow

Customer Handover Flow

Deal Won → Customer Record Created → Assigned to Support/Relationship Manager → Welcome Notification Sent → Renewal Reminder Scheduled
