# 🚀 AI Sales Agent System

An intelligent AI-powered sales automation system that captures leads, qualifies them, scores them, automates responses, and helps sales teams convert leads efficiently.

---

## 📌 Features

- Lead Capture (Website, WhatsApp, Manual, API)
- AI Chat / Auto Response
- Intent Detection (Pricing, Demo, Support, etc.)
- Lead Qualification
- Lead Scoring
- CRM Pipeline Management
- Follow-up Automation
- Human Handoff
- Quotation / Proposal Generation
- Analytics Dashboard

---

## 🧠 System Flow

Lead Capture → AI Response → Intent Detection → Qualification → Scoring → Pipeline Stage → Action → Follow-up → Conversion

---


## 🧩 Core Modules

1. Lead Capture  
Capture leads from forms, WhatsApp, APIs, or manual entry.

2. AI Conversation Engine  
Auto replies, intent detection, and dynamic conversations.

3. Lead Qualification  
Collect business needs, budget, timeline, and decision authority.

4. Lead Scoring  
Score leads based on behavior and interest level.

5. CRM Pipeline  
Manage stages like New, Contacted, Qualified, Proposal, Won, Lost.

6. Automation Engine  
Follow-ups, triggers, and notifications.

7. Human Handoff  
Assign leads to sales team when required.

8. Dashboard  
Track leads, performance, and conversions.

---

## 🔁 Example Logic

IF lead asks for pricing → send pricing + mark warm  
IF lead requests demo → mark hot + notify sales  
IF no reply in 3 days → send follow-up  
IF score > 80 → assign to senior sales
