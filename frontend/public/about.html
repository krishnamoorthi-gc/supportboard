About OnlyPOA

A modern support desk platform built to help teams manage customer conversations faster and smarter.

💡 Who We Are

OnlyPOA is a customer support platform that brings email, live chat, and team collaboration into one unified inbox. We help businesses respond to customer queries quickly, keep track of conversations, and deliver better support experiences — all from a single dashboard.

🚀 What We Do

Our platform lets support agents manage inboxes, assign conversations, automate replies with AI, and track team performance — without switching between multiple tools. Whether you're handling email threads, live chats, or internal notes, OnlyPOA keeps everything organized in one place.
