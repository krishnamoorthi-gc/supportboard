Data Deletion Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Your Right to Data Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data that SupportDesk has collected and stored. We are committed to honoring your data deletion requests in compliance with applicable data protection laws including GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant regulations.

2. How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data through any of the following methods:

  • Email: Send a request to privacy@onlypoa.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  • In-App: Navigate to Settings > Account > Delete My Data
  • Written Request: Send a written request to our business address

3. What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, we will delete the following data:

  • Your account profile information (name, email, avatar)
  • Conversation history and messages
  • Contact records and associated metadata
  • Uploaded files and attachments
  • Custom fields and form submissions
  • Activity logs related to your account

4. Data We May Retain

Certain data may be retained even after a deletion request for the following reasons:

  • Legal obligations: Data required for tax, legal, or regulatory compliance
  • Fraud prevention: Minimal data necessary to prevent fraud or abuse
  • Aggregated data: Anonymized, non-identifiable analytics data
  • Backup retention: Data in encrypted backups for up to 30 days after deletion

5. Processing Timeline

We will acknowledge your deletion request within 3 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days. If additional time is needed, we will notify you of the reason and expected completion date.

6. Third-Party Data

When we delete your data, we will also instruct any third-party processors who have received your data to delete it. This includes email service providers, cloud storage, and analytics services.

7. Consequences of Deletion

Please be aware that data deletion is permanent and irreversible. After deletion:

  • Your account will be permanently deactivated
  • You will lose access to all conversation history
  • Any active integrations will be disconnected
  • Team members will no longer see your agent profile

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us at privacy@onlypoa.com.
