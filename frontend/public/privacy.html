Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Introduction

SupportDesk ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our customer support platform accessible at www.onlypoa.com.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, password, and optional profile information such as avatar and role.

Communication Data: We store messages, emails, and conversations exchanged through our platform between support agents and customers, including attachments and metadata.

Contact Data: Information about your customers including names, email addresses, phone numbers, company details, and custom fields you define.

Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with the platform, including pages visited, features used, IP address, browser type, and device information.

Integration Data: If you connect third-party services (email, WhatsApp, social media), we process data from those integrations to provide our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our customer support platform
  • To process and manage support conversations across channels
  • To send email replies and notifications on your behalf
  • To provide AI-powered features such as reply suggestions, summarization, and auto-classification
  • To generate reports and analytics about support performance
  • To improve our platform and develop new features
  • To communicate with you about service updates and changes
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats

4. AI and Automated Processing

Our platform uses AI services (powered by Anthropic's Claude) for features including reply suggestions, conversation summaries, sentiment analysis, and auto-classification. Conversation data sent to AI services is processed according to our data processing agreements and is not used to train AI models.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the platform (cloud hosting, email delivery, AI processing)
  • Your Integrations: Services you connect (email providers, WhatsApp, social media platforms)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or court order
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • JWT-based authentication with token expiration
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular security reviews and updates

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (see our Data Deletion Policy)
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies and local storage to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@onlypoa.com.
